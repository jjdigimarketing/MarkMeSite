Case Study • Shorts + Ads

Vintage Clothing Brand — 0 to 500k+ views in 30 days

Instagram Reels & TikTok • Organic + boosted posts

500k+ total views 1.2k+ new followers 4 viral Reels (>50k views)

A local vintage seller was posting randomly with no real system. We came in, shot simple iPhone clips, and turned them into a weekly flow of Reels and TikToks focused on outfits, drops, and “behind the rack” content. Their page went from quiet to constantly getting discovered on Explore and For You.

  • Planned 3–4 short-form videos per week built around their actual inventory.
  • Hook-focused editing (fit checks, reactions, “what sold today”).
  • Boosted top-performing videos with small daily ad budgets.

Result: Consistent reach, constant new eyeballs on the brand, and way more DMs asking about pieces the day they drop.

Case Study • Local Business

Pasadena Café — 3× profile visits in 6 weeks

Instagram Reels • In-feed posts • Story repost system

3× profile visits +42% follower growth Multiple 10k+ view Reels

This café had good vibes in person but their feed didn’t show it. We focused on “what it feels like to be here” — drinks being made, busy weekends, and quick videos of staff and regulars. The idea was simple: make the page feel like you already know the place before you ever visit.

  • Shot short clips of drinks, food, and the line during peak hours.
  • Edited into fast, 8–15 second Reels with trending sounds.
  • Created a system to repost people’s Story tags for free extra reach.

Result: More people hitting “Directions” from the profile, more new faces walking in, and a feed that actually matches how the café feels in real life.
